Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

FadeFlow uses cookies and similar technologies to provide a better experience when you use our platform. This policy explains what cookies we use and why.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use a web application. They help us remember your preferences and improve the way the platform works for you.

What We Use Cookies For

  • Authentication: To keep you signed in to your account securely during your session.
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences within the platform.
  • Performance: To help the platform load faster and operate smoothly.
  • Analytics: To understand how features are being used so we can improve the product.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized access to accounts.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function. These cannot be turned off.
  • Functional cookies: Used to remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the platform.

Managing Cookies

Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies or be notified when a new cookie is set. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the platform.

Third-Party Cookies

Some of our third-party service providers (such as hosting and analytics services) may also set cookies. These are subject to the respective third parties' privacy policies.
